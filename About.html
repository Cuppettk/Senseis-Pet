About Sensei's Pet

Senseis pet is a program of necessity. It was designed primarily for use by Assistant Language Teachers working abroad as a tool to brighten up what is often monotonous, dull, and routine language practice.

This program, however, is certainly usable by many other's in a situation where the only way to thoroughly memorize something is through repitition. If you're going to have to repeat something over and over again, you might as well make it fun.

The various tools should help streamline the process from lesson planning to execution.
